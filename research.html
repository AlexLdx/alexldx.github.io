Research Interests

Within the field of algebraic geometry, my research focuses more specifically on various aspects of the theory of linear algebraic groups over an arbitrary field.

    • Alegebraic groups, pseudo-reductive groups, unipotent groups;
    • Cohomological theories;
    • Central simple algebras, quadratic forms;
    • Cohomological invariants.

Alexandre Lourdeaux

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the International Center for Mathematics of SUSTech (Shenzhen, China)[cite: 92, 95].

My research interests focus on Linear Algebraic Groups and their Galois Cohomology, specifically through the study of Cohomological Invariants[cite: 92, 95].

In the vast field of algebraic geometry, my work explores the symmetries and transformations underlying mathematical phenomena, often arising as automorphism groups of structures such as quadratic forms or central simple algebras.

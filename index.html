About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the International Center for Mathematics of SUSTech (Shenzhen, China).

My research interests are Linear Algebraic Groups and their Galois Cohomology, specifically through the subject of Cohomological Invariants.

I completed my PhD at University Lyon 1 (Institut Camille Jordan) under the supervision of Philippe Gille.
